About Me

I am an undergraduate AI researcher at Chungnam National University, specializing in natural language processing with a focus on instruction-based language models and retrieval-augmented systems. My research interests include sentence compression, summarization, and developing efficient methods for controlling text generation. I have published work in prestigious venues such as ACL Findings and NAACL Findings, and gained international research experience through an internship at AIST in Tokyo, Japan.
